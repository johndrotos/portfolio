Leadership and Hobbies

Double Discovery Center Residential Coordinator

Last summer, I worked as a Residential Coordinator at the Double Discovery Center at Columbia University, where I helped run the center's Summer Academy. I prepared first-generation students from low income and under-resourced communities with the skills necessary to succeed in a college environment, and I ensured the well-being and safety of all students. I also facilitated communication among all Summer Academy stakeholders including students and their families, DDC staff, and Columbia staff. The Summer Academy was a big success, and I am proud to have helped provide the students with a fulfilling and rewarding pre-college experience.
